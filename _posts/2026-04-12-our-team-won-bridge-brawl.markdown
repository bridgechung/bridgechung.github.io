My team and I won Kai's Bridge Brawl!! This has to be my best win in bridge to date, and I'm so happy. Really, my teammates were fantastic and they carried me over the finish line.

Side note: if you see anyone complaining that this format is "not bridge," well they can go kick rocks. David Burn even points out that it strictly *is* bridge, as the scoring can be determined by the regulating authority, which is Kai. And basically he's introduced even *more* decisions than we would usually have to think about during a bridge deal.
